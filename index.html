Exploer Animal World

A zebra is an African equine with distinctive black and white stripes that provide camouflage and social recognition.

Bears are large, powerful mammals with shaggy fur and a varied diet.

A wolf is a wild canine known for its intelligence, social behavior within packs, and its role as an apex predator in many ecosystems.

The peacock, a large, colorful pheasant, is renowned for the male's extravagant tail feathers, which it displays in a spectacular courtship dance.

Meerkats are small, social mongooses known for their upright posture as they scan for predators.
